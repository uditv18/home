Summary

Following a B.Sc. degree in Physics, Geology and Mathematics, I recently completed my coursework and thesis for my M.Sc. degree in Computational and Integrative Sciences at Jawaharlal Nehru University (final result awaited). I now aim to apply this experience to research on immunocompromised states, leveraging data-driven approaches to uncover novel insights and contribute meaningfully to current studies.
